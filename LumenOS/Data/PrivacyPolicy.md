# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: June 10, 2026**

Welcome to LumenOS. We take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## 1. Information We Collect
LumenOS is a privacy-focused utility application.
- **Personal Information**: We **do not collect** any personally identifiable information such as names, phone numbers, email addresses, or geographic locations.
- **Device Permissions**:
    - **Camera/Flashlight**: Used only for the flashlight function and torch sync barrage feature. We do not capture or upload any photos or videos through the camera.
    - **Haptic Feedback**: Used to enhance the application's interactive experience.
- **Usage Data**: We may collect anonymous diagnostic data (such as crash logs) to help us optimize app performance.

## 2. Use of Information
The anonymous data we collect is used only for:
- Ensuring the stable operation of the application.
- Improving product features and user experience.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We may use third-party payment providers (such as Apple App Store) to process subscriptions and purchases. Relevant payment information is subject to Apple's privacy policy, and we cannot access your credit card information.

## 4. Data Security
Since we do not collect personal information, there is no risk of personal data leakage. We strive to protect the security of the application itself.

## 5. Children's Privacy
Our application does not collect any information from children under the age of 13.
