# Product Introduction - LumenOS

LumenOS is an all-in-one light and shadow tool designed specifically for iOS users, blending extreme flashlight control with creative LED scrolling barrage features. Whether you're looking for keys in the dark, cheering for your idol at a concert, or setting the mood at a party, LumenOS is your ideal companion.

## Core Features

### 1. Intelligent Flashlight
- **Precision Control**: Supports stepless brightness adjustment to meet all-scenario needs from dim reading to high-intensity searching.
- **Multiple Modes**:
    - **Standard Mode**: A stable lighting source.
    - **SOS Mode**: International standard distress signal to keep you safe in critical moments.
    - **Strobe/Party Mode**: Rhythmic flashing effects to instantly ignite the atmosphere.
- **Dynamic Touch**: Unique knob-style UI design that simulates the tactile feel of real physical controls.

### 2. Creative Barrage
- **Personalized Customization**: Supports custom text, scrolling speed, and color.
- **Visual Effects**:
    - **LED Matrix**: Retro dot-matrix style that perfectly simulates physical LED screens.
    - **RGB Glow**: Supports vibrant rainbow-colored text effects.
    - **Dynamic Backgrounds**: Built-in romantic hearts, twinkling stars, aurora borealis, and more.
- **Torch Sync**: Barrage scrolling can be synchronized with the rhythm of your phone's flashlight, creating the ultimate live support effect.

### 3. Premium Design
- **Extreme Aesthetics**: Midnight Blue and Lumen Gold brand color palette, paired with delicate diffuse-light UI.
- **Haptic Feedback**: Deeply adapted to the system's Haptic engine, providing real feedback with every knob turn.
